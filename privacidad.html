Last updated February 08, 2026



This Privacy Notice for StickerSwap ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Download and use our mobile application (StickerSwap), or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice
Use StickerSwap. StickerSwap is a social networking mobile application designed for sticker collectors. It enables users to manage their personal collections, connect with other collectors through a geolocation-based matching system, exchange missing and duplicate stickers, and engage via integrated chat and community forums. Key Features: Personal Inventory Management: Real-time tracking of personal stock, including stickers owned, duplicates, and missing items. Swipe-to-Connect System: A discovery interface to find and match with nearby collectors based on proximity. Match & Instant Messaging System: Integrated tools to coordinate and confirm independent swaps through direct communication. Community Discussion Forums: Dedicated spaces for collectors to share tips, news, and engage with the community. Premium Subscription Tier: Advanced features and enhanced functionality available through a paid subscription model. Real-time Notification System: Instant updates on matches, incoming messages, and community activity. Customizable User Profiles: Personalized accounts featuring avatars and individual collector data. The application leverages geolocation services to bridge the gap between digital management and physical exchange, connecting users by geographic location to facilitate safe and efficient in-person sticker swaps.
